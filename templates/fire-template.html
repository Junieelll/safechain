II. DETAILED INCIDENT REPORTS
1. November 21, 2024 – Virginia St., Joan of Arc
- Property: 2-story residential rooftop (partially burned).
- Affected: 3 families.
- Cause: Faulty electrical wiring.
2. August 11, 2024 – Tagumpay St.
- Property: 10 informal settler houses (light materials).
- Casualties: 1 senior (female), 1 adult (male, 52), 1 PWD, 33-yr.
  female.
- Total Affected: 85 individuals.
- Cause: Illegal electrical connections.
3. April 13, 2024 – Gloria St.
- Property: Dressmaking shop.
- Affected: 6 families, 4 members with minor burns.
- Cause: Candle left unattached to plastic + overheated sewing
  machine.
III. CASUALTIES AND DAMAGES SUMMARY
- Casualties Total: 6
- Injured: 4
- Houses Affected: 23
- Families Evacuated: 15
IV. ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS
1. Electrical Issues: Faulty wiring, illegal connections, and
    unattended appliances (fans, tools).
2. Fire Hazards: Open flames in proximity to flammable materials.
3. Structural Vulnerabilities: Use of light materials in informal
    settlements.
V. REMARKS
- High-Risk Areas: Informal settlements (e.g., Tagumpay St., Gloria
  St.) lack fire safety infrastructure.
- Affected Families: Sta. Barbara St., etc., require assistance (e.g.,
  housing, livelihood).
- Community Awareness: Lack of fire prevention education in
  marginalized areas.
VI. RECOMMENDATIONS
1. Electrical Safety Campaign:
   - Free inspections for informal settlers.
   - Penalize illegal connections.
2. Fire Prevention Workshops:
   - Train residents on proper use of appliances and emergency
     response.
3. Infrastructure Upgrades:
   - Regulate use of fire-resistant materials in informal settlements.
4. 24/7 Monitoring:
   - Deploy BDRRMC teams to high-risk zones during peak fire
     seasons.
